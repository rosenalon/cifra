Director

Alon Rosen (Bocconi University and Reichman University): Alon's main research focus is cryptography and its interplay with computational complexity. He is also interested in the construction of efficient cryptographic schemes with provable security guarantees.

Associate Directors

Giulio Malavolta (Bocconi University): Giulio's research focuses on mathematical aspects of asymmetric cryptography and in the intersection with quantum computing. He is primarily interested in constructing new cryptographic schemes from well-founded computational assumptions.
Emmanuela Orsini (Bocconi University): Emmanuela's main research area is cryptography, with a focus on secure multiparty computation and zero-knowledge constructions. Her aim is to design efficient general-purpose protocols as well as specific solutions for privacy-preserving applications. Her interests extend to post-quantum cryptography, algebraic coding theory, and computational algebra.
